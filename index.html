Corona Situation

Urbanization

Geography matters in propagation of viruses. Urbanization undoubtedly has created new opportunities and soaring of economy but compact settlements can act as petridish for viruses. Viruses can find large number of hosts and vectors in urban hubs. The only method that seems to work is to prevent the spread, perhaps drawing the line for such microbes is arduous. To flatten the curve, flattening of population density curve is important. It is both challenge and opportunity for urbanism in 21st century.
